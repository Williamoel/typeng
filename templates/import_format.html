Import Format

TXT

Use three required fields: word, part of speech, Chinese meaning. An example sentence can be added as an optional fourth field.

abandon	verb	放弃；遗弃
ability	noun	能力
close	adjective	近的；亲密的
close	verb	关闭
abandon	verb	放弃；遗弃	The company decided to abandon the old plan.

CSV

CSV can include a header, or use the same columns without a header.

word,part_of_speech,meaning,example_sentence
abandon,verb,放弃；遗弃,The company decided to abandon the old plan.
ability,noun,能力,She has the ability to solve complex problems.
